
Welcome to CleanSweep's documentation!

=====================
CleanSweep
=====================

CleanSweep is a tool to find single-nucleotide variants along the entire genome of a specific strain in a 
plate swipe (also known as plate sweep, plate metagenomes, or mixed communities).
